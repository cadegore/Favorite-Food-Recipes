Beef Ramen Recipes

Description

A bowl of ramen with beef, most people in China loves it.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 ounce) package beef-flavored ramen noodles
  • 1 cup thinly sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • Â½ cup frozen shelled edamame
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Sriracha sauce, or more to taste
  • 1 (4 ounce) beef top sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste

Directions

  1. Cook ramen in a saucepan according to package directions
  2. adding mushrooms, edamame, soy sauce, and sriracha in the last 2 minutes of cooking.
  3. Add steak in the last minute of cooking.
  4. Top servings with cilantro and, if desired, additional Sriracha sauce.
